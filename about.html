Hello!

I'm Suyeon.

I was born and raised in Seoul, the capital of South Korea. I am majoring in industrial design at Sungshin Women's University in Seoul, South Korea. Right now, I am studying contemporary communication at LCC International University, located in Klaipėda, Lithuania, as an exchange student. I have been interested in design since high school and have been working in various fields, including visual design, motion graphics, and 3D. While I primarily focus on simple and modern styles of work, I explore a variety of designs, from minimalistic to elaborate, to broaden my design spectrum.
